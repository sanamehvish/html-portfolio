The Best Movies according to Sana

My top 3 movies of all time ...!!!

Taare Zameen Par

It is a 2007 Indian drama film about an 8-year-old boy with "dyslexia" who finds success through ART.

Super 30

Based on the life of Patna-based mathematician Anand Kumar who runs the famed Super 30 program for IIT aspirants in Patna.

MS DHONI-The Untold Story

A story of Dhoni's rise from a young cricketer in Ranchi to the most successful captain of the Indian cricket team.
